The Philosophy Behind Our Work

What is Karm Yog?

Karm yog is ancient wisdom rooted in the Bhagavad Gita that says that the most sacred act is to give your whole self to what is right and needed, without asking what returns to you. Not service as sacrifice but service as identity.

Karm

कर्म · ACTION

Every action we take writes the world forward. Karm is not just labour — it is the full weight of our choices, our duties, and the responsibility we carry for those whose names we may never know.

Yog

योग · UNION

Yog is alignment — between who you are and what you do. When your work flows from something deeper than ambition or applause, it ceases to deplete you. It becomes the very thing that makes you whole.

"You have a right to perform your prescribed duties, but you are not entitled to the fruits of your actions."

Bhagavad Gita · Chapter 2, Verse 47

Karm Yog at Upekkha

At Upekkha, Karm Yog is not just a belief — it is the way we work. We engage in social initiatives with full commitment, thoughtful planning, and humility, focusing on doing the work well rather than chasing visible results.

Our approach prioritizes learning from communities, responding to real needs, and building systems that create long-term impact. By embracing Karm Yog, we strive to serve with clarity, act without ego, and contribute to change.
